One Line About Me

I'm a fourth year PhD student at California Institute of Technology (Caltech), supervised by Prof. Philip F. Hopkins. I work on theoretical astrophysics and cosmology, and specifically on galaxy formation and evolution.

My Research

My research revolves around the formation and evolution of galaxies and the nature of dark matter. Several topics on my radar are:

  • Seeding & Evolution & Demography of early quasars (active supermassive black holes)
  • Galaxy formation in the early Universe; Mock observations for the next-gen facilities (e.g., JWST, Roman, LSST)
  • Explore alternative dark matter models to the WIMP-like cold dark matter model (my thesis research)
