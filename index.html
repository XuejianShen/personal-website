Hi! This is Xuejian Shen's personal website!

I'm a graduate student in Physics at Caltech. My research interests are cosmology, dark matter physics and galaxy formation physics.

  • Learn more

Dark Matter

Studying the astrophysical impacts of alternative dark matter models, including self-interacting dark matter, dissipative dark matter and ultra-light dark matter. I incorporate particle physics models of dark matter with cosmological simulations to study their impacts in the non-linear regime of structure formation of the universe.

  • Learn more

High Redshift Galaxies and AGNs

Studying the evolution of the galaxy and AGN populations at high redshift. I focus on statistical properties of galaxies and AGNs and provide predictions for observations conducted with next generation instruments.

  • Learn more

Dust Modelling

Modelling the dust attenuation in galaxies through emipirical/analytical/radiative transfer methods.
